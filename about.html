Who We Are

Lighting Everything, the SOLID way.

SOLID is the lighting brand of Solid State Lights, based in Bhilwadi, Maharashtra. We design and manufacture a complete range of LED fixtures — from recessed COB downlights and slim LED panels to track, spot and surface lights — built around our promise of “Lighting Everything.”

Every product is engineered with strong die-cast metal bodies, premium COB & SMD chips and anti-glare optics, so it performs beautifully and lasts for years. We supply homes, offices, showrooms, hotels and architectural projects across the country.

  • 120+ models across downlights, panels, track & spot lights
  • Strong metal structures with efficient heat dissipation
  • Quality-tested batches with a 2-year warranty
