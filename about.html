About Us

Leaders in LED Indoor Lighting Solutions Since 2004.

Established in 2004, Solid State Lights is a leading LED indoor lighting manufacturer and supplier in India, specializing in innovative, energy-efficient, and cost-effective lighting solutions for modern residential, commercial, retail, hospitality, office, and industrial spaces.

With vast experience in the LED lighting industry, we have built a strong reputation for delivering durable, high-performance, and aesthetically designed indoor lighting products that meet evolving architectural and interior lighting trends. Our focus is to provide premium-quality LED lighting solutions that combine modern design, low power consumption, high lumen efficiency, long operational life, and reliable performance.
