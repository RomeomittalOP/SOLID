About Us

Leaders in LED Indoor Lighting Since 2004.

Solid State Lights was established in 2004 — at a time when LED technology was still finding its footing in India. We were among the early manufacturers who believed that good lighting isn't just functional — it shapes how a space feels, how people work in it, and how long a building owner's maintenance bills stay low.

Two decades later, that belief still drives everything we make.

We manufacture a focused range of indoor LED lighting — COB downlights, panel lights, track lights, surface lights and architectural fixtures. Our products are used by architects, interior designers, builders, contractors and government projects across India. Not because we promise the most, but because we consistently deliver what we promise.

We are not a catalogue company that imports and rebrands. Our products are built with selected components, tested for performance, and backed by people you can actually reach when something needs sorting.

If you're specifying lighting for a project — whether it's a single office floor or a large hospitality fit-out — we're straightforward to work with, our pricing is honest, and our range covers most requirements from a single source.
